About ABH

There have been many personalities who have fought for the freedom of this country. While growing up we have heard the names of many such stalwarts. Every kid in every nook and corner of this country has by-heart the names of the prominent ones; namely, Mahatma Gandhi, Pt.Jawaharlal Nehru, Subash Ch. Bose,Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev, Lal, Bal, Pal, Aurobindo Ghosh, Dadabhai Naoroji, GK Gokhale and many more. Rightly so, they deserve every bit of our remembrance. They have fought in their own strides. Some peacefully and some as revolutionaries. But in my recent studies about the freedom struggle, I have found out that, there were innumerous contribution by many a people, in getting us freedom.
